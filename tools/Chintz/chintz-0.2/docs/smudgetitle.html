Smudge: The Title Tag

< Title> allows Pages, Frames, Boxes, Buttons, Lines, CheckBoxes and RadioButtons to be provided with a label. The Title may contain spaces and new line characters (\n). In the cases of Lines, the Title may additionally have its own Geometry and a Boldface setting.
